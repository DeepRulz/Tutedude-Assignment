Welcome to Laundry Wallah

Laundry Wallah is a modern, tech-enabled laundry and dry-cleaning service designed to simplify urban living. Through a user-friendly app, customers can easily schedule convenient, on-demand pickups and deliveries directly to their doorstep, eliminating the need for trips to the laundromat. The service offers professional cleaning for a wide array of items, including everyday clothing, delicate garments, and large household items like linens and rugs. Committed to quality and sustainability, they utilize professional, eco-friendly cleaning methods and provide transparent, affordable pricing across their service areas in major Indian cities, backed by reliable seven-day-a-week customer support.
